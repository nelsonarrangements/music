PistonLink

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

PistonLink is an iPadOS and iOS app developed by Garrett Nelson ("we," "us," or "our") that helps organists manage stop registration profiles, browse curated hymn registrations, and turn sheet music pages hands-free. This Privacy Policy describes what data PistonLink accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data We Do Not Collect

PistonLink does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party. All information you create or import within the app — including registration profiles, sheet music PDFs, and organ configuration data — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash-reporting services, or any other technology that sends data off your device.

Camera and Face Data

PistonLink requests access to your device's front-facing camera and TrueDepth sensor solely to power the hands-free page-turn feature. The app detects eye blinks in real time to advance or retreat sheet music pages. Camera and facial geometry data are processed entirely on-device using Apple's ARKit framework and are never stored, recorded, or transmitted anywhere.

MIDI and Bluetooth

PistonLink communicates with supported organs over MIDI (via USB or Bluetooth MIDI). MIDI messages sent and received are used only to read and write organ piston registrations in real time and are not logged or stored beyond the current session.

Files and Storage

PDF sheet music you import into PistonLink is stored in the app's local on-device container. Registration profiles and organ configurations you create are also saved locally. None of this data is backed up to any server operated by us; iCloud backups, if enabled by you in iOS Settings, are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

PistonLink does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of PistonLink after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us through the contact page on this website.
