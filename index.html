How Arrangements are Made Updates and Changes FAQ

Timeline

How arrangements are made
Step 01

Choosing a Hymn

Each hymn is chosen out of the need for a certain type of arrangement or for a certain style of arrangement. Certain hymns are more condusive to certain styles than others.

Step 02

Arranging & Composition

During this step, the arrangement is determined. This is where the harmony, rhythm, and tembre of the song is adjusted for the arrangement prior to the performance of the arrangement.

Step 03

Recording

It's during this step that the final adjustments are made to the arrangement and the arrangement is recorded. This is also where any last-minute improvisation happens that is condusive to the composition.

Step 04

Publishing & Sheet Music

Arrangements are cleaned up, fine-tuned for listening, and then published on this website, alongside the YouTube Channel. Shortly following, if Sheet Music is beneficial for the piece, it is then created over a period of about 2-3 weeks to ensure authenticity, as well as intended sound from the arrangement that was performed in the recording.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

This is all of the top questions about Nelson Arrangements.

What is the video release schedule?

The video release schedule varies, but it is intended that a video will be uploaded every three weeks.

Why are so few pieces of Sheet Music available?

Sheet Music is an arduous process to write, and ensure that it is accurate with the arrangement. Each arrangement is a memorized performance that must then be transferred to sheet music afterwards. As a one-person show, this can take a while to get through with the backlog of arrangements.

How long as Nelson Arrangements been around?

The arrangements were started between 2021 - 2023, but the website and YouTube Channel were created in late 2023.

How long is an arrangement's product cycle?

It is typically around 2 weeks.

Are there plans for more than one Organ arrangement?

Yes, there are multiple that are slated to be re-recorded to ensure higher listening quality prior to publication.

Does Nelson Arrangements have any Social Networking accounts besides the YouTube Channel?

Yes, there is an Instagram Account: nelsonarrangements.
