Behind the music

How arrangements are made

01

Choosing a Hymn

Each hymn is chosen out of the need for a certain type of arrangement or for a certain style. Some hymns are more conducive to certain styles than others.

02

Arranging & Composition

The harmony, rhythm, and timbre of the hymn are shaped for the arrangement style — all before a single note is recorded.

03

Recording

Final adjustments are made and the arrangement is recorded. This is also where any last-minute improvisation happens that serves the composition.

04

Publishing & Sheet Music

Arrangements are fine-tuned and published here and on YouTube. Where sheet music is beneficial, it follows over roughly 2–3 weeks to ensure authenticity to the recorded performance.

Common questions

Have a question?

Everything you want to know about Nelson Arrangements.

What is the video release schedule?

The schedule varies, but a new video is intended every three weeks.

Why is so little sheet music available?

Sheet music is an arduous process — each arrangement is a memorized performance that must be transcribed afterward. As a one-person operation, working through the backlog takes time.

How long has Nelson Arrangements been around?

Arrangements were started between 2021–2023. The website and YouTube channel launched in late 2023.

How long is an arrangement's production cycle?

Typically around two weeks from start to publication.

Are more organ arrangements planned?

Yes — several are slated to be re-recorded to ensure higher listening quality before publication.

Is Nelson Arrangements on social media besides YouTube?

Yes — Instagram: nelsonarrangements.
