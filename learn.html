The Organ Loft

Techniques for the bench, from registration to reharmonization

For many musicians, the transition from the piano bench to the organ console feels like stepping into a cockpit without a flight manual. The Organ Loft is designed to bridge that gap. Here, you will find resources focused on the mechanics of the instrument—from conquering "pedal paralysis" to understanding registration—specifically tailored for the ward organist. The goal isn't just to play the notes, but to lead the congregation with confidence and intent.
