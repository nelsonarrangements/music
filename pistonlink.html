PistonLink

The app that takes the guesswork out of organ stop registration — built for the bench, not the manual.

In Development

What it does

Everything you need at the console

Registration Profiles

Create and save named registration profiles that capture the full stop state across General and Divisional pistons for the Great, Swell, and Pedal divisions. Deploy them directly to the organ's memory slots via MIDI SysEx — no more guessing at the console.

Registrant Library

A built-in library of curated stop suggestions for LDS hymns, with verse-by-verse variations. Browse a hymn, find the right sound for each verse, and save it straight to any organ profile — ready for Sunday.

Hands-Free Page Turns

Import any PDF into the built-in sheet music library and turn pages without lifting your hands from the keys. Winking with the right eye advances the page, and the left eye goes back — keeping you in the music where you belong.

MIDI SysEx Integration

Built around the Rodgers 790C as its first supported organ, PistonLink communicates directly with the instrument over MIDI, writing your saved registrations to the organ's own memory pistons with a tap.
