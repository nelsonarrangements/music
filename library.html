The Nelson Arrangements Library

Curated pedagogy and creative resources for the modern church musician.

This library serves as a digital extension of my workshops and arranging process. Here, you will find technical guides, registration and other 'cheat sheets,' and deep dives into the mechanics of hymns––designed to help you move beyond the printed page and play with greater intention and worship.


The Organ Loft


Techniques for the bench, from registration to reharmonization.

These resources are designed specifically for the LDS ward organist. Whether you are transitioning from the piano or looking to spice up familiar hymns, these guides focus on practical, 'Sunday-ready' techniques.
