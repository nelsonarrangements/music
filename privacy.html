Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This policy describes how nelsonarrangements.com handles information when you visit the site or use the contact form. This site does not run ads, does not use tracking analytics, and does not sell data.

Information You Submit

When you submit the contact form, the information you enter — name, email address, inquiry type, and message — is transmitted to a Cloudflare Worker and then forwarded to the site owner via Resend, a transactional email service. This data is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not retained by any automated system beyond normal email.

Cloudflare Turnstile

The contact form uses Cloudflare Turnstile to distinguish human visitors from automated bots. Turnstile is an invisible challenge — it runs silently in the background when you submit the form and does not display a CAPTCHA or require any action from you.

Cloudflare may collect telemetry signals (such as browser characteristics and interaction patterns) to assess whether a submission is automated. This data is processed by Cloudflare according to their privacy practices. You can review how Cloudflare handles Turnstile data in the Cloudflare Turnstile Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Local Storage

This site stores a small preference in your browser's local storage to remember whether you have dismissed the newsletter signup banner. No cookies are set by this site, and no tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Third-Party Links

Pages on this site link to YouTube and other external services. Once you leave nelsonarrangements.com, those services' own privacy policies apply.

PistonLink

The iOS and iPadOS app PistonLink has its own privacy policy. See the PistonLink Privacy Policy for details.

If this policy is updated, the revised version will appear at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
